# AI_CONTEXT

## Project

ActionPilot

Tagline:

From Intention to Completion

---

## Product Category

AI Execution Companion

---

## Core Problem

People frequently miss deadlines because traditional productivity tools rely on passive reminders and do not actively help users complete work.

---

## Product Goal

Help users complete goals before deadlines are missed through:

* Planning
* Monitoring
* Accountability
* Risk Detection
* Recovery Support

---

## Core Differentiator

ActionPilot is not a task manager.

ActionPilot continuously monitors execution and proactively intervenes when users are at risk of missing deadlines.

---

## Agent Architecture

* Deadline

* Effort Estimate
* Risks

### Planning Agent

* Tasks
* Milestones

---

### Execution Monitoring Agent

Outputs:

* Progress Analysis
* Monitoring Signals

---

### Risk Agent

Outputs:

* Risk Score
* Risk Level

---

### Recovery Agent

Outputs:

* Recovery Plan

* Scope Reduction Suggestions
