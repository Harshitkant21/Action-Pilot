# Future Roadmap

## Overview

This document outlines the future evolution of ActionPilot beyond the hackathon MVP.

The MVP focuses on helping users complete goals through planning, accountability, risk detection, recovery planning, and proactive execution support.

Future versions will expand the platform into a comprehensive AI-powered execution operating system.

---

# Product Evolution Strategy

ActionPilot will evolve through three major stages:

### Stage 1

Execution Companion

# Long-Term Vision

Most productivity tools help users organize information.

ActionPilot aims to help users consistently execute and achieve outcomes.

The long-term vision is to create an AI-powered execution companion that understands goals, monitors progress, adapts plans, predicts risks, and helps individuals achieve meaningful results with less stress and greater consistency.
